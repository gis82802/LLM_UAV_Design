
Structure and Airframe Module - Comprehensive Structural Parameters by Scenario
Based on：無人機智慧化輔助設計平台原型建構計畫

Scenario一 熱帶颱風後應急通訊
- Wingspan：≥ 3.5 m
- Airframe Length × Width：≤ 1.5 m × 1.5 m
- Dry Weight：≤ 5.5 kg
- Structural Material Density：≤ 2.0 g/cm³（e.g.：碳纖維複材、Kevlar、防鹽霧陽極氧化鋁）
- Flexural Modulus：≥ 130 GPa
- Allowable Stress：≥ 600 MPa
- Payload Bay Dimensions：≥ 250 × 200 × 100 mm
- Max Payload Weight：≥ 5.5 kg
- CG Offset Tolerance：±30 mm
